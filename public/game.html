How to Play Tunisian Rummy

  • Form sets (same rank, different suits) or runs (sequential rank, same suit).
  • You can meld with 3+ cards.
  • Discard to end your turn.
  • First to empty hand wins.
  • Right-click your hand to meld.
  • Left-click to draw/discard.
  • Play with friends or CPU.
